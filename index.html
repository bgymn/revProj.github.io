It's getting "S.H.I.T.T.Y." out there...

It's getting "So Hot It Totally Tortures You" out there. Climate change is on the rise. Students for Sustainable Infrastructure wants you to join our cause in curbing these changes. With climate change the socio-economic disparities experienced in many places around the US will widen as well. The focus of this movement is to raise public awarness and lobby legislators at the municipal, county, and state levels to prepare for the increasinly harmful UHIs that are observed among urban spaces here in Colorado and at the national scale.
